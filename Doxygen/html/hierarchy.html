Untitled Jump Game
The core concept of this game takes inspiration from the classic hit mobile game doodle jump. The player will control their character to move left and right of the screen while the character jumps automatically. The goal is to reach a new high score by climbing the level as high as possible while avoiding dangers from enemies. If the player falls they will immediately fail.
